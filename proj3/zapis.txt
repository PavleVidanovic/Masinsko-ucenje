Dataset Name: Water Quality

Link: https://www.kaggle.com/adityakadiwal/water-potability

Description: This dataset contains 3276 observations with 9 input variables and 1 target variable. 
The input variables include water quality parameters such as pH, hardness, sulfate, chloride, and other parameters. 
The target variable indicates whether the water is potable (safe for consumption) or not. 
This dataset is a good choice for ANN classification as it provides a real-world application 
for classification models and is of potential interest to those in the environmental and public health industries.
